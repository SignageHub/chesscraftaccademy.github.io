About Chess Craft Academy

Uganda's leading center for chess education and training

Welcome to Our Physical Academy

Chess Craft Academy is a dedicated chess training center located in the heart of Kampala. We provide professional, in-person chess instruction for players of all ages and skill levels.

Founded in 2015, our academy has trained thousands of chess enthusiasts, from complete beginners to national champions. Our experienced coaches are passionate about sharing their knowledge and helping you improve your game.

We believe in the power of face-to-face instruction, personalized feedback, and the community that forms when chess enthusiasts gather together in one place.

Core Chess Skills We Teach

Master these essential skills to improve your chess game

Opening Skills

Learn principles and develop pieces for a strong start. Master various opening strategies and understand the ideas behind them.

Middlegame Strategy

Plan and coordinate your pieces to outmaneuver opponents. Learn how to create and execute effective plans in complex positions.

Endgame Technique

Master king and pawn endings to secure the win. Learn essential endgame principles that convert advantages into victories.

Calculation & Visualization

Think ahead and visualize variations multiple moves deep. Improve your ability to calculate complex sequences accurately.

Evaluation & Planning

Assess positions and make a long-term plan for victory. Learn how to evaluate any chess position and formulate appropriate strategies.

Game Analysis

Learn to review your games to identify mistakes and improve. We teach you how to analyze your play to grow faster.
